Favorite Car Type

I love JDM cars. I like the flashy colors and lights. They also have a lot of mods you can get for them.

Toyota GT86

My first JDM car I am going to get is the GT86. I love the way the look and how cheap they are. A brand new 2024 cost $36,000 and a nice used one is $20,000. I want to mod JDM cars with exterior mods. I would get it lowered, chambered, wide bodied, and add underglow. A Rocket Bunny kit is $4000. It comes with the wide body and wing.

Mazda Miata

Miatas are a cool small JDM car. It cost $30,000 brand new and used one is about $20,000. I would get it wide bodied, custom headlights, and a wing.

Nissan Skyline

My all time dream JDM car is the Skyline. The Skyline cost $121,000 brand new and used is around $60,000. I would lower and chamber it.
